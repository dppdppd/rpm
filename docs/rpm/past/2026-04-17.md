## Part 2 Key Discoveries
- **Latent parser bug** surfaced by the T7 bats suite: a `DONE` or
  `CANCELLED` heading's `:ID:` drawer was overwriting `CUR_ID` for
  the prior actionable heading in both `task-capture.sh` and
  (initially) `score-natives.sh`. Fixed both: terminal-state branch
  scores/flushes the prior heading and resets `CUR_HEADING=""`; the
  `:ID:` matcher now guards on `[ -n "$CUR_HEADING" ]`. Inline
  comments in both files capture the invariant.
